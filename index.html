Truist Park and Braves Culture

Braves fans are known for their loyalty, energy, and love for the game. Truist Park provides a modern baseball atmosphere while still honoring the team’s long tradition. Whether it is a big home run, a close playoff game, or a summer night at the ballpark, being a Braves fan is about more than baseball — it is about community.
