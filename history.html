Atlanta Braves History

A quick look at one of baseball’s most storied franchises.

Early Origins

The Braves franchise is one of the oldest in professional baseball. Over the years, the team has played in multiple cities and built a long legacy of competition and success.

The Move to Atlanta

The Braves moved to Atlanta in 1966 and quickly became a major part of the city’s sports identity. The team developed a strong fanbase throughout the South and became known for both star power and winning seasons.

Iconic Braves Eras

1990s Dominance

The Braves became one of baseball’s top teams, winning division titles and building an unforgettable rotation and roster.

Championship Success

The Braves earned championship recognition through strong team culture, star performances, and consistent postseason appearances.

Modern Braves

Today’s Braves continue to compete with exciting young talent, powerful hitters, and one of the best baseball environments in the league.

Legendary Players

Braves history includes many unforgettable players who helped shape the team’s identity. Known for skill, leadership, and big moments, these players left a lasting mark on both the franchise and the sport.
